Important Appointment Booking Information

If you are aged 18 to 39 years, you cannot currently book a booster vaccination. More information will be available soon about how 18 to 39 year olds will get their vaccine. If you can't find a date, venue and time that suits, come back later as new clinics are being added daily. The national vaccination helpline will be unable to offer alternative appointments as they use the same online booking system. If you've already received your flu vaccination and are now eligible to book for your COVID-19 booster, please do not book an appointment via the online portal, you should call the national helpline on 0800 030 8013 who will be able to make an appointment on your behalf.
